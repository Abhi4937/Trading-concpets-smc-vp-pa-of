What is Volume Profile?
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OjPXvptaCYw

Ever wondered what this histogram next to your chart is? That's the volume profile and it's one of the most powerful trading tools out there. It shows how much trading volume happened at each price level, not just over time. So instead of looking at timebased volume bars, you are seeing where big traders actually traded the most. The high volume areas show where the market agreed on value. Price often makes reactions there. Low volume areas show rejections. Price usually moves fast through them. This helps you spot key support and resistance zones created by institutional traders. In short, you are not guessing. You are trading where real money traded.
