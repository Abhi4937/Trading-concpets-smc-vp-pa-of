## How charts work in these notes
Every lesson uses a **real TradingView chart chosen to fit that specific concept** (matched symbol, timeframe, and a historical date where the pattern actually appears clean), **with my markings drawn on it** — entries, stops, targets, zones, arrows. **No reused/generic chart across notes.**
